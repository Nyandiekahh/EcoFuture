Volunteer With Us

We're always looking for passionate individuals to join our volunteer team. Here are some of the ways you can get involved:

Community Cleanup Volunteer

Participate in our regular beach and park cleanup events to help keep our local environment clean.

Time commitment: 3-4 hours, monthly opportunities

Education Ambassador

Help deliver our environmental education programs in schools and community events.

Time commitment: 4-6 hours per week, training provided

Administrative Support

Assist with office tasks, data entry, and event planning.

Time commitment: Flexible, 2-10 hours per week

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to EcoFuture Alliance?

You can donate through our secure online donation portal, by mailing a check to our office, or by contacting us to discuss other options like monthly giving or legacy gifts.

Are my donations tax-deductible?

Yes, EcoFuture Alliance is a registered non-profit organization and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

How can my company partner with EcoFuture Alliance?

We offer various corporate partnership opportunities including sponsorships, employee volunteer programs, and cause marketing campaigns. Please contact our partnerships team for more information.

Do you offer internships or student positions?

Yes, we offer seasonal internships for students interested in environmental activism and non-profit management. Check our website or contact us for current opportunities.
