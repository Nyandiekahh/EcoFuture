About Us

Our Story

EcoFuture Alliance was founded in 2020 by a group of environmental scientists and community activists who recognized the urgent need for coordinated action on climate change.

What began as a small local initiative has grown into a dedicated network of volunteers, experts, and supporters working together to create sustainable solutions for our planet's most pressing environmental challenges.

Today, we operate across the Lower Mainland, engaging communities, businesses, and government in collaborative efforts to protect our shared environment.

Our team at the 2025 Vancouver Environmental Action Conference

Our Vision

We envision a world where environmental sustainability is integrated into every aspect of society, where communities thrive in harmony with nature, and where future generations inherit a healthy, resilient planet.

Education

Empowering people with knowledge about environmental issues and sustainable practices.

Innovation

Supporting creative solutions and new approaches to environmental challenges.

Inclusion

Ensuring that all voices are heard and that environmental benefits are shared equitably.

Action

Moving beyond awareness to implement tangible changes in policies and practices.
